How to Merge PDF Files in 3 Simple Steps

February 12, 2023

What You'll Need

  • PDF files you want to combine
  • Our PDF Tools

Step-by-Step Instructions

Step 1: Upload Files

Click "Choose Files" and select up to 20 PDFs from your device.

Step 2: Arrange Pages

Drag and drop files to set the merge order. Use the preview pane to verify.

Step 3: Download

Click "Merge PDFs" and save your combined file (processing takes 10-30 seconds).
