URL Safety: How to Spot Dangerous Links

January 21, 2023

Malicious links are responsible for 94% of malware infections. Here's how to protect yourself when clicking shortened URLs or unfamiliar links.

Warning Signs

  • Misspelled domains: "facebo0k.com" instead of "facebook.com"
  • Unusual subdomains: "facebook.login.security.com"
  • HTTP instead of HTTPS: Always look for the padlock icon

Safety Checklist

  1. Hover over links to preview the destination
  2. Use our URL Shortener to create trusted links
  3. Install a link scanner browser extension

Remember: Never enter passwords after clicking links in emails. Always navigate directly to sites.
